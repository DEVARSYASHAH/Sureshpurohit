About me

I am Suresh Purohit, an innovator and entrepreneur driven by a passion for creating impactful solutions and inspiring change. With over 23 years of experience across Business Review, Techno-Commercial Management, Project Management, Marketing, Strategy, and Innovation, I have built a career that blends vision with execution.

Holding a BE in Chemical Engineering and an MBA in Marketing, I have founded six startups and initiatives that reflect my belief in thinking without constraints. Alongside entrepreneurship, I enjoy engaging with communities as a Quiz Master, having conducted 30+ quizzes, and as a Visiting Faculty at the Faculty of Management Studies, The M. S. University of Baroda, where I mentor aspiring leaders.

My journey has also been shaped by contributions to the innovation ecosystem—whether as a Founding Member of the Vadodara Innovation Council, organizing team member of Maker Fest Vadodara, or through leadership roles with the Baroda Management Association and Rajpurohit Navyuvak Mandal Vadodara.

Over the years, my work has been recognized with honors such as the VNM TV Environment Excellence Award and by supporting Zydus in winning the “Best Business Unit in Innovation” award. I take pride in the fact that my expertise has benefitted leading organizations including Zydus, Sun Pharma, Emcure Pharma, Godrej, and many more.

At heart, I am a believer in creativity, collaboration, and continuous learning—qualities that fuel my mission to make a meaningful difference in business and society.
